The 7 Cardinal Virtues

1. Fillial Love

Filial Love, one of the cardinal virtues in DeMolay International, emphasizes the deep respect and affection that sons owe to their parents and guardians. It inspires members to honor the sacrifices and guidance provided by those who have nurtured and supported them throughout life. This virtue calls for expressions of gratitude, obedience, and care, reflecting the foundational role of family in shaping moral character and fostering a sense of responsibility toward others. Filial Love is a reminder of the enduring bonds and obligations within the family, which serve as a cornerstone for building a virtuous and compassionate society.

2. Reverence for Sacred Things

Reverence for Sacred Things is a key pillar of the Seven Cardinal Virtues of DeMolay, emphasizing deep respect for spiritual values and the divine. This virtue encourages members to honor their faith, uphold moral principles, and respect the beliefs of others, fostering a sense of unity and mutual understanding. It reminds young men to recognize the presence of something greater than themselves, guiding their actions with humility and purpose. By practicing reverence, DeMolays cultivate a life rooted in integrity and respect for both spiritual traditions and the sacred aspects of daily life.

3. Courtesy

Courtesy, a cardinal virtue of DeMolay International, represents the practice of respect, kindness, and consideration toward others in all interactions. It teaches members to uphold the dignity of every individual by showing politeness and understanding, regardless of differences in background or opinion. Courtesy is more than just good manners; it reflects a genuine commitment to fostering harmony and goodwill in personal and professional relationships. By embracing this virtue, DeMolays strive to create an environment of mutual respect and civility, embodying the principles of brotherhood and moral integrity.

4. Comradeship

Comradeship is a cornerstone of the Seven Cardinal Virtues of DeMolay, symbolizing the bond of brotherhood and mutual support among members. It encourages young men to build meaningful friendships based on trust, loyalty, and shared experiences. Through comradeship, members learn the value of standing together in times of joy and challenge, fostering a spirit of unity and cooperation. This virtue inspires DeMolays to embrace teamwork, offer help when needed, and create a lifelong network of support and friendship that extends beyond the organization.

5. Fidelity

Fidelity is a vital element of the Seven Cardinal Virtues of DeMolay, representing unwavering loyalty and faithfulness to one's beliefs, commitments, and relationships. It calls on members to remain true to their word, honor their obligations, and uphold the trust placed in them by others. Fidelity inspires young men to act with integrity, ensuring that their actions align with their values and principles. This virtue strengthens bonds of trust and exemplifies the importance of dedication to family, friends, and the ideals of DeMolay.

6. Cleaness

Cleanliness is one of the Seven Cardinal Virtues of DeMolay that signifies purity of body, mind, and spirit. It encourages members to lead lives free from harmful habits, negative thoughts, and unethical actions, promoting a commitment to self-respect and personal discipline. This virtue extends beyond physical cleanliness to include maintaining a positive character and fostering wholesome relationships. By embracing cleanliness, DeMolays strive to present themselves as individuals of integrity, setting a positive example for others and contributing to a healthier, more honorable community.

7. Patriotism

Patriotism, a cardinal virtue of DeMolay International, reflects a steadfast love and loyalty to one's country. It encourages members to honor the sacrifices of those who have fought for freedom and justice while actively contributing to the betterment of their nation through responsible citizenship. This virtue instills pride in national heritage and inspires members to uphold the ideals of liberty, equality, and service to the greater good. Through patriotism, DeMolays affirm their commitment to building a just and prosperous society, recognizing that true loyalty to one’s country is shown through ethical actions and a dedication to its improvement.
